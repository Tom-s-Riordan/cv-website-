The End Is Here.

A brutal, unflinching imagining of a war wherein both sides feel morally justified by religious belief. Any behavior, any tactic, no matter how blood thirsty or murderous is condoned by political and military leadership. The ultimate goal justifies the ultimate means. Through the eyes of a front line solider, this story is told. He struggles with the morality of war on behalf of God. These are the battles of revelation, the battle against the forces of the Antichrist but each side claims its opponent is that enemy. How could a standard issue solider know the truth? Could he be fighting on behalf of the Antichrist

The True Untold Story Of America's Fist and Only All Black, Special Operations Unit

A story a about family: The brotherhood between men in combat and the love of family left behind. We honor the history of these gallant Americans as they strive toward victory against all odds.

72 Hours To Kill

Once a year, an underground gaming event takes place wherein a normal person is marked without thier knowledge. Then, the hunt begins. Unnamed, faceless assasians hunt that person, each vying for the coveted “kill” and the prize money that comes with it. While private viewing parties rage, overseen by the game’s creator LOS, millions are wagered on every imaginable outcome; the kill, the injuries, weapons, over/ under lines and even on the person’s survival.
