Tom Riordan CEO and Technical Advisor

Tom served in the US Army as a Medic with the 25th Infantry Division and the 914th FSH.
Tom has worked as a Technical Advisor on such shows as: The Rookie, Castle, NCIS, Westworld, Project 12, Agents Of Shield and Station 19.
His stunt credits include The Mandalorian, Agents of Shield, Extant, 24, Touch, and The Event.
Tom is also a published writer and graduate of the WGA Veterans Project. He is the author of Killbox (published by American Gothic Press) and has penned multiple screenplays which have been produced, including episodic BlackBoxTelevision along with Project 12, Winner of Best Feature at the Roswell International Film Festival.
